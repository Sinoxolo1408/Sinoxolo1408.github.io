Online purchase prediction

This is an application built to predict whether a customer will make a purchase when online shopping.

Technologies used Python Jupyter Java

The prediction of whether or not a customer will eventually make a purchase while using an online shopping portal is made based on the pattern of pages visited on the website by the customer, along with certain analytical features, the time of the week and year and other features. Machine learning tacniques such as Logistic regression, Neural Networks and Support vector machines are used together to produce the best prediction.
